About Goldstar

We Goldstar Company Services and Transport LLC, are an oilfield service company, operating out of Pecos, TX. We mainly focus on the transportation trade managing vacuum trucks, flatbed trucks, and hotshot. We also cover pressure wash crews, roustabouts, and contract pumpers.
